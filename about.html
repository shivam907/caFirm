About Us

Kedia Dhandharia & Co.

was established in 1988 with the vision to help our clients achieve their goals through a people services and solutions. Having a diverse clientele ranging from modern E-Commerce operators to the traditional family oriented business set-ups, we provide a one stop solution for our clients starting from accounting and assurance to tax advisory and management consultancy.

The firm having its registered office in Kolkata is trying to spread its wings in other major cities as well. The biggest strength of the firm is its ideology and integrity coupled with the transparency that it maintains with its clients.

The firm comprises a team of young professionals as well as of experienced minds working together in different verticals, each headed by respective domain experts. The protocols and processes which have been refined over the years help to ensure timely and effective communication and strict adherence to deadlines.
